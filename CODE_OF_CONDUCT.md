# Code of Conduct

FriendlyTerminal is built to make technical tools feel more welcoming. The
project community should feel the same way.

## Our standards

Contributors are expected to be respectful, patient, constructive, and mindful
that people have different backgrounds and levels of terminal experience. Give
actionable feedback, assume good intent, and focus disagreement on ideas and
technical tradeoffs.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, sexualized conduct, threats,
doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when conduct harms the community. Reports should be made
privately using the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when public reporting
would expose personal or sensitive information.

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as practical. Anyone
enforcing this policy must avoid conflicts of interest and protect reporters
from retaliation.
